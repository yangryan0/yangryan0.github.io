Quiz (Java/Android)

I did this project in Summer 2017 while I was learning Android programming on the side. This is a simple quiz app that supports True/False questions, and can also tell you the correct answer when you tap the "cheat" button. In addition, when tapping the cheat button, any subsequent answers will be accompanied by a message stating that cheating is bad. The "next" and "previous" buttons allow you to navigate through the questions.
